Pheme - UWA Access Management System

Welcome to Pheme

Pheme is the UWA Access Management System, a place where you can set a password for your central university account giving you access to a range of services.

Please select one of the following.

  • Students

    You are an enrolled student. The eight-digit student number on your Campus Card starts with a 1 or 2.

  • Staff

    You are a staff member or closely associated with the University. The eight-digit staff number on your Campus Card starts with a zero.

  • Visitors

    You are temporarily associated with the University. The five digits of your Person ID on your Campus Card will have 5, A, B, F, L, M, R or V as a prefix.
