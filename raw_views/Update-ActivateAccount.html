Email and Calendaring (ECS)
Email Address
The University provides all staff with an official UWA email address.

Your University email address is based upon information from the Human Resources system. Depending on your name, you may have more than one option to choose from.

If you have another preferred given name, please specify this via the ESS.

If you have another surname in the HR system (e.g. through marriage, etc.), and wish to use this in your email address, go to ESS to choose that as your preferred surname.
Email Destination
  • The Email Destination should correspond to a personal mailbox, ideally on a UWA mail server.
  • This mailbox will have the format mailbox@somewhere.uwa.edu.au, where "somewhere" could be "admin", "cyllene", or another UWA sub-domain.
  • Mail may be delivered to a mailbox on these specific mail domains: @health.wa.gov.au, @agric.wa.gov.au, @csiro.gov.au, @lei.org.au, @perthfestival.com.au, @rcswa.edu.au, @nteu.org.au, @guc.edu.au, @stgeorgescollege.com.au and @telethonkids.org.au
  • To deliver mail to other non-UWA mail server please contact ITS Client Services via the IT Service Desk.
